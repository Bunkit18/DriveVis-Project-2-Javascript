DriveVis

Welcome to DriveVis, a place where you can get to grips and learn to drive, from the comfort of your own home!

Here at DriveVis, you'll be shown a video of a driver, driving in different conditions and areas. Some areas may be rural, others urban.

Sun, Rain, wind or snow, you'll encounter it all. You'll learn how to safely manouver the road like a pro, without the cost!

How to use DriveVis

On the right-hand side, there will be an overlay of buttons including: Indicators, Gears Accelerator and Break. As the video progresses, all you need to do is press the buttons in good time to your own judgement.

If your timing is accurate, you'll see a green 'Great!' appear. If you miss it or are a little too late, you'll see a red 'missed!' or 'too late!' comment. It's as simple as that, so practice hard!

Whenever you're ready to start, just click Begin!
